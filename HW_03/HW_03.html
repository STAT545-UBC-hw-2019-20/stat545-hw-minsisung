Task Option 5

How is life expectancy changing over time on different continents?

Since there are lots of lifeExp data in each continent, we need to get the mean of the lifeExp for each continent and plot its changing over time.

gapminder %>% 
  group_by(year, continent) %>% 
  summarize(mean_lifeExp = mean(lifeExp)) %>% 
  ggplot(aes(year, mean_lifeExp, group = continent, colour = continent)) +
  geom_line()
